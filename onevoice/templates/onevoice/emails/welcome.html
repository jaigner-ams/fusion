How One Voice Works

Step 1: We mail postcards to dentists in your target area.
Step 2: Our CSR team calls the dentists on your behalf.
Step 3: Appointments are booked directly to your calendar.
Step 4: You visit the dentist and build your business.

Best Practices

Follow up the same day the appointment is made. This shows the prospect you're serious and professional.

Reaffirm the appointment the day before your visit, or the morning of for early appointments.

Pro Tip: Bring donuts or submarine sandwiches for the dental office staff. It goes a long way in making a great first impression!
